About Trees17

Trees17 is a Bangladeshi initiative dedicated to celebrating and preserving the country's magnificent trees. Our mission is to raise awareness about the importance of trees in our ecosystem and inspire action to protect them.

Founded in 2017, we have documented over 5,000 unique trees from all districts of Bangladesh, highlighting their ecological, cultural, and historical significance.
